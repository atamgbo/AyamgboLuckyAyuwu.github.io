Hi! My name is Atamgbo, you can call me Ata. I am a Data Analyst passionate about working with organizations that want to make effective use of their data to enable evidence-driven decision making or share insights with new audiences. I currently work as a Quality Analyst responsible for analyzing and strategizing the reliability, functionability, performance and quality of products and services.
