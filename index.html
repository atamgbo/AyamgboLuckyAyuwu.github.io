Projects

In Data Analytics, understanding the problem and the results you need to achieve is the first and most important step of the process. The next steps would be setting a clear metric, gathering data, cleaning data, analyzing and mining data, interpreting result and then presenting your findings to the stakeholders to help make better business decisions.

Data Analytics helps answer some important questions such as "What happened?", "Why did it happen?", "What will happen next?" and "What should be done about it?". It supports business decisions and drives global change, it makes predictions and tells us what the result would be by taking a certain action.
